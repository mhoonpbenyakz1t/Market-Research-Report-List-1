<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fingerprint Identification Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Module</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Module</li><li>Ultrasonic Fingerprint Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fingerprint Identification Module market is segmented into three main types: capacitive, optical, and ultrasonic fingerprint modules. Capacitive fingerprint modules use electrical signals to capture fingerprints, providing high accuracy and robustness against contaminants. Optical fingerprint modules utilize light to capture images of fingerprints, offering cost-effectiveness and ease of integration. Ultrasonic fingerprint modules employ sound waves to penetrate the skin, delivering superior accuracy and security even in challenging conditions. Each type serves varying applications across consumer electronics, security systems, and biometric authentication.</p></p>
