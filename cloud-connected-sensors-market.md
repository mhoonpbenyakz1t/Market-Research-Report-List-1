<p><p>The Cloud Connected Sensors market can be categorized into Traditional Wireless Technology and LPWANs Technology. Traditional Wireless Technology includes established standards like Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, which offer high data rates but limited range and battery life. In contrast, LPWANs (Low Power Wide Area Networks) provide long-range connectivity with low power consumption, making them ideal for IoT applications. LPWANs facilitate efficient data transmission over vast distances, supporting numerous sensors and devices, which makes them increasingly popular in various sectors.</p></p>
